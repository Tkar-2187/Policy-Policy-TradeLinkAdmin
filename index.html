Privacy Policy for TradeLink Admin App

Last Updated: July 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the TradeLink Admin mobile application (“App”). This app is intended for authorized administrators only.

1. Information We Collect

To ensure the proper functioning of the App, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your email and password for secure sign-in via Firebase Authentication.
  • Device Token: A Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token to send push notifications about orders and other critical events.
  • Security and Financial Data You Manage: As an admin, you input and manage data such as a 4-digit security PIN, KBZ bank account numbers, KPay QR codes, nicknames, price lists, and daily balances. This data is stored securely in Firestore and Firebase Storage.
  • Order Information: The app processes user order data from Firestore, including order details, status, and confirmation screenshots that you upload.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected and managed information to:

  • Authenticate your access to the Admin App.
  • Enable you to manage user orders, verify payments, and provide service confirmations.
  • Deliver important notifications related to administrative tasks.
  • Secure sensitive sections of the app with your PIN.

3. Biometric Authentication

With your explicit consent, the app uses your device's native biometric features (e.g., fingerprint, face unlock) for secure login, facilitated by the local_auth package. Your actual biometric data (fingerprint or face scan) is never accessed, collected, or stored by our App or servers; all verification is handled securely on your device.

4. Data Storage and Security

We are committed to protecting all data. We leverage the robust security of the Firebase platform:

  • All data, including admin-managed details and user orders, is stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage.
  • Access to data is restricted by Firebase Security Rules to ensure only authorized admins can view and manage information.
  • All communication with Firebase services is encrypted.
  • The 4-digit PIN provides an additional layer of security for sensitive operations within the app.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information. Your data is not shared with outside parties except:

  • With trusted third parties (e.g., Firebase by Google) who assist in operating our application under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • When we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law or a legal process.

6. Administrator Rights

As an administrator, you have the right to:

  • Access and manage the data necessary for your role as outlined in this policy.
  • Reset your password. For security reasons, account deletion must be requested through our designated support channel.

7. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will update the policy modification date above. We may notify you through the app or by other means of any significant changes.

8. Contact Us

For questions or privacy-related concerns regarding the Admin App, please contact the development team through the designated support channel.

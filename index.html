# Privacy Policy for TradeLink Admin App **Last Updated:** July 22, 2025 At TradeLink, we are dedicated to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you utilize the TradeLink Admin mobile application (“App”). By accessing the App, you acknowledge and agree to the practices outlined herein. ## Information We Collect TradeLink Admin App collects certain information to ensure its proper functionality and to provide a seamless user experience. When you sign in, we gather your email address and password through Firebase Authentication to secure your account access. If you enable biometric authentication, such as fingerprint or face recognition, the App uses the `local_auth` package to verify your identity locally on your device, without storing or collecting your biometric data. We also obtain a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token from your device to deliver push notifications related to orders and significant events. As an admin, you may set a 4-digit security PIN to protect access to sensitive areas, such as the KBZ Details Management page, which is stored securely in Firestore. Additionally, the App allows you to manage and store KBZ bank account numbers, KPay QR code images, and associated nicknames, all saved in Firestore and Firebase Storage. Data entered for price calculations, including various values and totals, is associated with specific dates and stored in Firestore. Similarly, daily balance calculation data you input is maintained in Firestore. The App also retrieves and enables you to manage user orders from Firestore, including viewing details, updating statuses (e.g., from “pending” to “ongoing”), and uploading confirmation screenshots. ## How We Use Your Information The information collected by the TradeLink Admin App is utilized to enhance your experience and support its core functions. Your account details are used to authenticate your identity and manage access to the App’s features, ensuring only authorized administrators can perform critical tasks. Order-related data enables you to accept, manage, and complete user orders, verify payments, and send confirmation details to users. Push notifications keep you informed about new orders, status changes, and any promotional messages you choose to distribute. The security PIN and other measures are implemented to protect sensitive financial data within the App, adding an extra layer of security for your administrative duties. ## Information Sharing TradeLink is committed to maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to third parties without your consent. However, we may share data with trusted third-party providers, such as Firebase (operated by Google), who assist in operating the App and delivering services, provided they adhere to strict confidentiality agreements. Disclosure may also occur when required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of TradeLink, its users, or others, in accordance with legal obligations. ## Data Security Protecting your data is a fundamental priority for TradeLink. We employ the robust security features of the Firebase platform, including Firebase Authentication, Firestore Security Rules, and Firebase Storage Security Rules, to safeguard your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
